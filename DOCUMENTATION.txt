What it does: Calculates your age in seconds.

What output should look like:

All inputs should be their numerical values (i.e. July would be 7).
 
What day of the month were you born?
8
What month were you born?
5
What year were you born?
1985
Your age in seconds is: 1085250111

How to run script:

1: Download file.
2: Open File Explorer, and navigate to the downloaded file.
3: Right-click the file, and select "Run with Powershell"
